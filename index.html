      about

      This infographic creates a thought-provoking, critical, and even amusing visualization of 119 core job skills to help everyday users understand the language of the job market and ultimately help them find a job.

      The Open Skills Project is a database that catalogs jobs and skills, asking what are jobs called, and what skills are relevant to those jobs? Read more about it below!

      Vision Statement, Data at Work http://dataatwork.org/about/ Importance and expertise level data courtesy of ONET https://www.onetonline.org/

      legend

      circle size = importance, scaled 1—5

      • 1 = irrelevant
      • 2 = convenient
      • 3 = important
      • 4 = necessary
      • 5 = critical

      circle color = expertise-level, scaled 0—7

      • purple = little or no experience
      • red = novice
      • yellow = competent
      • green = expert
      • blue = mastery
